# SALES_PLAYBOOK.md — Outreach Strategy & Scripts

> Core principle: lead with the **business problem**, not the technology. Personalize every touch.

---

## Channels & daily targets

### 1. LinkedIn (primary)
- 30 new relevant connections/day (founders, CEOs, CTOs, COOs, product/ops heads).
- 10 conversations/day.
- Target: 2 discovery calls booked.

### 2. Cold email
- 20 emails/day.
- Personalized, focused on a specific business problem.

### 3. Upwork
- Premium AI profile; bid selectively on high-fit AI/automation projects.

### 4. Agency partnerships
- Become the AI implementation partner for existing dev/marketing agencies.

### 5. Referrals
- Past colleagues, Tatvic network, previous clients, friends.

---

## Ideal customer profile (ICP)

- **Segments:** startups, SMEs, mid-size businesses.
- **Industries:** healthcare, finance, retail, manufacturing, logistics, marketing, SaaS, enterprise ops.
- **Buyers:** Founder, CEO, CTO, COO, Product Manager, Operations Head.
- **Trigger signals:** manual/repetitive workflows, slow reporting, high support volume, missed calls/leads, document-heavy processes, stalled analytics.

---

## Message templates (starting points — always personalize)

### LinkedIn connection note
> Hi {FirstName} — I help {industry} teams automate {specific workflow} with AI so they {business outcome}. Would love to connect and share a couple of ideas relevant to {Company}.

### LinkedIn follow-up (after accept)
> Thanks for connecting, {FirstName}. Quick one — is {specific pain, e.g. "handling support tickets manually"} something your team is dealing with? We recently helped a {similar company} cut {metric} by {result}. Happy to share how, no pitch.

### Cold email (problem-first)
> **Subject:** {Company} + a faster way to handle {process}
>
> Hi {FirstName},
>
> Most {industry} teams still handle {process, e.g. invoices / support / reporting} manually — it's slow and error-prone. We build AI that {specific outcome, e.g. "processes documents automatically and flags exceptions"}.
>
> For {Company}, that could mean {tangible benefit}. Worth a 15-min call to see if it fits?
>
> — Mayank, NorthArc

### Discovery call → next step
> Based on what you shared, here's where AI would move the needle: {1–2 outcomes}. I'll send a short scoped proposal — deliverable, timeline, price — by {date}.

---

## Sales stages

1. **Connect** → 2. **Conversation** → 3. **Discovery call** → 4. **Scoped proposal** → 5. **Pilot / paid engagement** → 6. **Delivery** → 7. **Testimonial + case study** → 8. **Referral / expansion.**
