# PROJECT_CONTEXT.md — Brand, Vision & Positioning

## Company vision

NorthArc is an **AI Engineering, Data Science, and Intelligent Automation company** focused on helping businesses transform operations through Artificial Intelligence.

We are **not** a generic software development agency. Our mission is to solve real business problems using AI, Machine Learning, Data Science, Generative AI, Intelligent Automation, and Analytics.

Our positioning is always **premium, enterprise-ready, and business-focused**.

---

## Brand story

The name **NorthArc** combines two ideas:

**North** — direction, guidance, purpose, clarity, "true north," strategic decision-making.

**Arc** — transformation, growth, journey, innovation, connection.

Together, **NorthArc represents guiding businesses through their journey of intelligent transformation.**

**Tagline:** *Connecting Intelligence to Impact.*
