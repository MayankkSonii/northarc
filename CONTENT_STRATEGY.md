# CONTENT_STRATEGY.md — LinkedIn & Blog

> Goal: build credibility and thought leadership so prospects trust NorthArc before the first call.
> Cadence: **weekly LinkedIn content** (minimum). Blog follows once Resources section is live.

---

## Guiding principles

- **Teach, don't pitch.** Share how AI solves real business problems.
- **Outcome-first.** Lead with the result, then explain the how.
- **Specific > generic.** Real numbers, real workflows, real trade-offs.
- **Founder voice.** Draw on Mayank's hands-on experience (OCR, predictive models, LLM apps, analytics, ex-Tatvic).

---

## LinkedIn content pillars

1. **Business outcomes** — "How we cut X by Y using AI" style breakdowns.
2. **Education** — demystify AI/GenAI/agents for non-technical leaders.
3. **Behind the build** — how a specific AI solution actually works (Document AI, Voice AI, RAG).
4. **Industry angles** — AI use cases per vertical (healthcare, finance, retail, SaaS…).
5. **Founder POV** — opinions, lessons, myths, what's overhyped vs real.
6. **Proof** — demos, mini case studies, testimonials (as they arrive).

### Post formats
- Short insight posts (hook + 3–5 lines + takeaway).
- Carousels (step-by-step or before/after).
- Demo videos / GIFs of solutions.
- "Problem → approach → result" mini case studies.

---

## Starter topic ideas

- "Your team is doing document processing by hand. Here's what that actually costs."
- "AI agents vs chatbots — what the difference means for your support team."
- "5 workflows every SME should automate first with AI."
- "Why most 'AI projects' fail (and how to scope one that won't)."
- "What a Voice AI receptionist does to your missed-call rate."
- "RAG in plain English: give your team answers from your own docs."
- "Predicting churn before it happens — a practical walkthrough."
- "Build vs buy: when custom AI beats an off-the-shelf tool."
