# ROADMAP.md — Product & Business Roadmap

## Immediate priorities (in order)

1. **Land the first paying client.**
2. Improve the website after every client interaction.
3. Publish weekly LinkedIn content.
4. Create solution demos.
5. Collect testimonials.

---

## Phase 1 — Credibility & first client (now)

- Polish the website continuously (enterprise feel, performance, SEO, accessibility).
- Fix outstanding site issues (see [TODO.md](TODO.md)) — especially data/privacy items before pushing traffic.
- Build 2–3 solution demos (e.g. Document AI, Voice AI, an internal knowledge assistant).
- Run the outreach engine (see [SALES_PLAYBOOK.md](SALES_PLAYBOOK.md)).
- Publish weekly LinkedIn content (see [CONTENT_STRATEGY.md](CONTENT_STRATEGY.md)).
- Convert first conversations → discovery calls → first paid engagement.

## Phase 2 — Proof & repeatability

- Turn first projects into real, verified case studies + testimonials.
- Productize the winning service package (clear scope, timeline, price).
- Establish a repeatable delivery process (intake → scoping → build → handoff).
- Launch Blog / Resources / Case Studies sections on the site.

## Phase 3 — Scale services

- Add first AI engineer(s); document delivery playbooks.
- Agency partnerships — become an AI implementation partner.
- Expand into new industries with proven verticalized offers.
- Careers page + hiring pipeline.

## Phase 4 — Products (services → product + services)

Long-term, NorthArc becomes a **product + services** company. Candidate SaaS:
- AI Customer Support Platform
- AI Voice Platform
- AI Document Processing
- AI Analytics Platform
- AI Knowledge Assistant

Approach: let recurring client problems reveal which product to build first.

---

## Long-term vision (5 years)

NorthArc becomes a globally recognized AI engineering company with:
- International clients
- A team of AI engineers
- Consulting **and** SaaS offerings
- Strong thought leadership
- Repeatable delivery processes
